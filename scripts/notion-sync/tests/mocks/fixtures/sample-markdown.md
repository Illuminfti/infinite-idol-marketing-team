# Ika Minami

The shameless protagonist who refuses to disappear.

## Background

Ika woke up outside the Eternal Stage with no memories, only to discover she had already qualified for the tournament through a viral performance she couldn't remember.

### Key Facts

- **Hair:** Pink gradient (rose roots to magenta tips)
- **Original Fans:** 47 dedicated viewers
- **Signature Move:** The diving tackle catch

## Personality

Ika is:

1. Confident without being arrogant
2. Shameless about her appeal
3. Genuinely caring beneath the bravado

> "If I'm going to use my body as a selling point, I'm going to OWN it."

## Relationships

- **Sora:** Her first friend at the Eternal Stage
- **Suiren:** Rival turned ally
- **Erina:** Primary antagonist
