## 1. Core Concept: Simp Wars

### The Frame

Ika doesn't need saving. She needs **entertaining**.

> "You want my attention? Earn it. I've got a whole server of simps competing for a single headpat. Show me what makes you special~"

The Discord becomes a competitive arena where:
- **Factions** compete for Ika's favor
- **Individuals** climb the Simp Leaderboard
- **Mini-games** award Devotion Points
- **Milestones** unlock fan service content
- **Chaos** is encouraged, not moderated away

### Why This Works

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Competition drives engagement** | Faction wars, leaderboards, bragging rights |
| **Fan service is the actual product** | Outfit reveals, art drops, voice clips as rewards |
| **Games are fun on their own** | Even if you never pre-reg, the Discord is entertaining |
| **Simping is already the culture** | We're just making it official and gamified |
| **Ika is confident, not desperate** | She's running the show, not begging for attention |

## 3. The Games (Actually Fun Stuff)

### 3.1 IKA GACHA SIMULATOR

A mock gacha you can pull in Discord. No real money, just vibes.

**How It Works:**
- Earn "tickets" through activity (messages, reactions, game wins)
- Use tickets on mock banner pulls
- Pull results posted publicly in #gacha-salt
- Collect character cards (Ika variants, other idols, items)
- Flex your collection, roast others' bad luck

**Banner Rotation:**
| Banner | Duration | Featured |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Debut Ika** | Week 1-2 | Base Ika + common items |
| **Swimsuit Ika** | Week 3-4 | Fan service variant (YES THIS IS THE POINT) |
| **Dark Mode Ika** | Week 5-6 | Yandere aesthetic variant |
| **RATE UP IS A LIE** | Final week | All variants, "boosted" rates (still trash) |

**Salt Generation:**
- #gacha-salt channel for posting pulls
- Bot roasts bad pulls automatically
- "Skill Issue" role for anyone who goes 100 pulls without SSR
- Community celebrates/mocks accordingly

### 3.2 WAIFU TIER WARS

Structured debates about who's best girl. Yes, we're leaning into it.

**Weekly Debate Topics:**
- "Ika vs [Competitor Game Waifu]: Who wins and why"
- "Best Ika outfit: Dark Mode vs Swimsuit"
- "Is Senpai even worth chasing?"
- "Yandere vs Tsundere: The eternal question"

**Format:**
1. Topic announced Monday
2. Factions submit champion debaters
3. Moderated debate in voice channel (recorded)
4. Community votes on winner
5. Winning faction gets Devotion boost

### 3.3 SIMP SPEEDRUN

Daily challenges with time pressure.

| Challenge | Goal | Time |
|-----------|------|------|
| **First Simp** | First to react to Ika's daily post | Instant |
| **Compliment Rush** | Most creative compliment in 60 seconds | 60s |
| **Fan Art Blitz** | Fastest sketch of daily theme | 15 min |
| **Lore Quiz** | Answer Ika lore questions fastest | 30s/question |

### 3.4 HEADPAT ROULETTE

Ika gives out ONE headpat per day. Literally just a random selection from active users.

- Join pool by reacting to daily message
- One winner selected randomly
- Winner gets special role for 24 hours
- Gets to ask Ika one question (answered by bot/team)
- Pure parasocial gambling, completely stupid, absolutely addictive

### 3.5 COSPLAY CLOSET

Vote on what Ika wears next.

**Weekly Outfit Vote:**
- Three options presented (with preview sketches)
- Community votes
- Winning outfit gets full art reveal on Friday
- Voters who picked winner get bonus Devotion

**Example Votes:**
- School Uniform vs Maid Outfit vs Gym Clothes
- Bunny Suit vs Cat Ears vs Shrine Maiden
- Battle Armor vs Formal Dress vs Pajamas

This is literally just fan service content drops disguised as democracy. And it works.
