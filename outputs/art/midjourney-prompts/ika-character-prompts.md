## Style Consistency Guidelines

### Always Include in Ika Prompts
- `pink gradient hair rose to magenta`
- `dark luxury aesthetic`
- `black and gold color palette`
- `elegant mature not cutesy`
- `premium anime idol`

### Always Avoid
- ❌ `cute`, `kawaii`, `chibi`
- ❌ `pastel colors`, `pink theme` (use dark luxury palette)
- ❌ `happy cheerful` (use confident, determined)
- ❌ Generic descriptions without her specific features

### Color Palette Emphasis
- **Primary**: Black, gold, rose pink (hair), magenta (hair tips)
- **Accents**: Deep jewel tones, minimal white highlights
- **Lighting**: Dramatic (not soft), high contrast
- **Mood**: Sophisticated, not sweet

## Variation Parameters

### For Different Moods
- **Confident Ika**: Add `powerful stance, commanding presence`

- **Playful Ika**: Add `mischievous smile, light energy, subtle tease`
- **Determined Ika**: Add `intense gaze, athletic ready pose, competitive fire`

### For Different Settings
- **Stage Performance**: Add `concert lighting, audience implied, performance energy`
- **Training**: Add `practice room mirrors, athletic wear variant, focused intensity`
- **Casual**: Add `off-stage moment, relaxed but elegant, intimate setting`
- **Promotional**: Add `key art composition, dramatic pose, marketing ready`

## Quality Control Checklist

Before approving any generated Ika art, verify:
- ✅ Pink gradient hair (rose to magenta) is clearly visible
- ✅ Dark luxury aesthetic maintained (not cute/pastel)
- ✅ Black and gold color scheme prominent
- ✅ Expression matches intended mood (confident/vulnerable/playful)
- ✅ Athletic build visible (not overly delicate or cutesy proportions)
- ✅ Heart motif accessories present (signature element)
- ✅ Lighting is dramatic and sophisticated
- ✅ Overall vibe is mature, not childish

### Common Issues to Flag
- Hair looks fully pink without gradient → Regenerate
- Aesthetic too cute/kawaii → Regenerate with stronger dark luxury emphasis
- Expression too generic → Add more specific emotional direction
- Colors too pastel → Emphasize black gold jewel tones
- Build looks too delicate → Emphasize athletic petite build
