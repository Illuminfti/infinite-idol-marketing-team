## Notes for Agents

### Referencing Approvals

When implementing approved items:

1. **Link to Approval**: Always reference the APPR-XXXX number in commits and logs
2. **Update Status**: Mark the item as implemented after completion
3. **Log Activity**: Record implementation in `logs/agent-activity.md`

### Learning from Approvals

- Review past approvals to understand what types of decisions get approved
- Note any conditions or modifications that were applied
- Use approval patterns to inform future escalation decisions

### Archiving

- Items older than 6 months may be moved to a separate archive file
- Coordinator handles archiving during monthly maintenance
