# Skill: Crisis Management

> **Purpose**: Handle threats, crises, and brand protection
> **Primary User**: The Shield (Agent 14)
> **Use When**: Responding to threats, managing crises, protecting brand

---

## Crisis Severity Levels

| Level | Description | Response Time | Escalation |
|-------|-------------|---------------|------------|
| **P0** | Active harm to community | Immediate | Human required |
| **P1** | Reputation threat | <4 hours | Coordinator + Human |
| **P2** | Potential issue | <24 hours | Monitor and document |

---

## Quick Response Playbooks

### Scam Alert
1. Document (screenshot)
2. Report to platform
3. Alert community
4. Track for victims
5. Post-incident review

### FUD Campaign
1. Assess validity
2. Determine respond/ignore
3. If respond: calm, factual, not defensive
4. Monitor sentiment shift
5. Document learnings

### Negative Viral
1. PAUSE - don't react immediately
2. Assess: valid criticism or misunderstanding?
3. Get human approval for response
4. Respond appropriately
5. Learn and prevent

## Communication Templates

### Scam Alert Post
```
Official Security Notice:

We're aware of [scam type] targeting our community.

Our ONLY official accounts: [list]
We will NEVER DM first asking for funds
We will NEVER ask for seed phrases

Report imposters. Stay safe.
```

### Crisis Response
```
Hey everyone,

We're aware of [situation]. Here's what we know:
[Brief facts]

What we're doing:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]

We'll update you as we learn more.
```
