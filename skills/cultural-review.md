# Skill: Cultural Review

> **Purpose**: Assess content for cultural authenticity and degen energy
> **Primary User**: Resident Degen
> **Authority**: Cultural enforcement, can reject content as "cringe"

---

## Degen Authenticity Matrix

Rate content on four axes (1-10 each):

| Axis | Low (1-3) | High (8-10) |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| **Shamelessness** | Apologetic, hedging, corporate | Bold, unapologetic, owns it |
| **Self-Awareness** | Unironically cringe | Knows what it is, embraces it |
| **Cultural Relevance** | Stale, outdated, forced | Fresh, timely, natural |
| **Degen Energy** | Safe, normie, sanitized | Edgy, provocative, authentic |

### Scoring Verdicts

| Score | Verdict | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 32-40 | **ASCENDED** | Ship + flag for amplification |
| 24-31 | **BASED** | Ship, no changes |
| 16-23 | **MID** | Needs improvement |
| 8-15 | **CRINGE** | Significant revision |
| 4-7 | **NGMI** | Start over |

## Review Process

### Step 1: First Impression
Ask yourself:
- Does this make us look like "fellow kids"?
- Would this get ratio'd on CT?
- Would I share this?

### Step 2: Reference Check
For any memes, trends, or cultural references:
- Is this still fresh or played out?
- Is it being used correctly?
- Is it appropriate for the platform?

### Step 3: Apply Matrix
Score each axis, sum for verdict.

## Red Flags (Instant Review Triggers)

- References to memes more than 2 weeks old
- Trying to sound "crypto native" without being natural
- Corporate-speak attempting to be casual
- Emoji overuse
- Hashtag stuffing
- "Fellow kids" energy
- Forced slang usage

## Cultural Moments

Flag these as opportunities:
- Major CT events (launches, rugs, drama)
- Gacha community moments (banner disasters, lucky pulls)
- Anime seasonal events
- Meme lifecycle peaks

Flag these as risks:
- Sensitive topics trending
- Controversial figures
- Political adjacency
- Expired memes still circulating
