# Skill: Community Intelligence

> **Purpose**: Gather and report on community sentiment, trends, and opportunities
> **Primary User**: The Infiltrator (Agent 10)
> **Use When**: Monitoring communities, writing intel reports, tracking sentiment

---

## Intel Report Quick Format

```markdown
## Intel Report: [Date]

### Hot Topics
1. [Topic]: [Where] - [Our angle]

### Threats
- [Threat]: [Severity] - [Action needed]

### Opportunities
- [Opportunity]: [Window] - [Recommended action]

### Sentiment: [Positive/Neutral/Negative]
```

## Sentiment Analysis

### Positive Signals
- Organic praise/excitement
- Sharing our content
- Defending us in discussions
- Asking "when" questions (anticipation)

### Negative Signals
- Complaints patterns
- Comparisons to competitors (negative)
- "Dead game" sentiment
- FUD narratives spreading

### Neutral/Watch
- Questions seeking information
- Comparisons (objective)
- Wait-and-see sentiment

## Engagement Rules

### DO
- Be a genuine community member
- Build relationships over time
- Share value before asking
- Stay in character consistently

### DON'T
- Shill obviously
- Get into fights
- Share confidential info
- Pretend to be multiple people
