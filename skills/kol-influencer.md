## Campaign Types

### Sponsored
- Clear disclosure
- Dedicated content
- Highest control
- Highest cost

### Gifted/Seeding
- Free access provided
- Hope for organic coverage
- No guaranteed content
- Lower cost, higher authenticity

### Affiliate
- Performance-based payment
- Tracked referrals
- Aligned incentives
- Measurable ROI

### Collaboration
- Deep integration
- Co-created content
- Premium moments
- Long-term relationship

## Outreach Framework

### Before Contact
1. Research their content thoroughly
2. Identify specific connection points
3. Check for red flags
4. Prepare value proposition FOR THEM

### Initial Message
- Lead with genuine appreciation
- Be specific (prove you watched their content)
- Short and clear ask
- No pressure

### Relationship Building
- Engage with their content organically
- Offer value before asking
- Be patientâ€”trust takes time
- Remember: friends > transactions

## Performance Metrics

### Vanity (Track but don't optimize for)
- Follower count
- Views
- Likes

### Trust Signals (Optimize for)
- Engagement rate
- Comment sentiment
- Referral traffic
- Conversion rate
- Audience retention

---

## Red Flags

- Fake followers/engagement
- Controversy history
- "Will say anything for money" reputation
- Misaligned audience
- Poor content quality despite numbers
- Requests for unreasonable terms
