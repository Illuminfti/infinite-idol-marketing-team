## The CT Domination Philosophy

CT is not social media. CT is a **24/7 global arena** where reputations are built, tokens are discovered, and communities form overnight. To win on CT:

1. **Be native** - Speak like you live here, not like you're visiting
2. **Add value** - Every post should entertain, inform, or provoke thought
3. **Move fast** - Trends die in hours, not days
4. **Build relationships** - CT is a network graph, not a broadcast channel

## Reply Guy Protocol

> *"The fastest way to grow on CT isn't posting‚Äîit's replying."*

### Why Replies Work

- Your reply appears to the original poster's entire audience
- High-quality replies get pinned, quoted, and remembered
- You build relationships with accounts larger than yours
- The algorithm rewards engagement

### Target Account Tiers

| Tier | Follower Range | Strategy |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **Whale** | 100k+ | Thoughtful, valuable adds only. Quality over frequency. |
| **Big Fish** | 20k-100k | Regular engagement, build recognition |
| **Peer** | 5k-20k | Mutual support, collaboration opportunities |
| **Rising** | 1k-5k | Early relationship building |

### Reply Formulas That Work

| Type | Formula | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Value Add** | Expand on their point | "This. And the second-order effect is..." |
| **Contrarian** | Respectful disagreement | "Interesting take. Counter-argument: ..." |
| **Data Drop** | Add relevant facts | "Numbers to back this: [stat]" |
| **Experience** | Personal relevance | "We saw this exact pattern when..." |
| **Humor** | On-brand wit | "The virgin [X] vs the chad [Y]" |
| **Question** | Engagement bait | "Genuine question: what about [edge case]?" |

### Reply DON'Ts

- Never shill your project in unrelated threads
- Never reply with just "This" or "So true"
- Never argue aggressively (ratio bait)
- Never reply to every post from same account (stalker energy)
- Never copy someone else's successful reply format exactly

### Daily Reply Targets

| Day Type | Target Replies | Focus |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| **Normal** | 10-15 | Mix of tiers, value-first |
| **Trending Event** | 20-30 | Concentrate on trending topics |
| **Quiet Day** | 5-10 | Quality over quantity |

## Ratio Risk Assessment

Before posting, ask:

1. **Is this punching down?** ‚Üí Don't post
2. **Could this be misread as serious?** ‚Üí Add clarity or don't post
3. **Are we picking a fight we can't win?** ‚Üí Don't post
4. **Does this make us look defensive?** ‚Üí Reframe or don't post
5. **Would Ika say this?** ‚Üí If no, reconsider

### Ratio Recovery

If ratio'd:
1. **Don't delete** (unless factually wrong or harmful)
2. **Don't argue** (feeds the ratio)
3. **Acknowledge if fair criticism** ("fair point, we'll think on this")
4. **Move on quickly** (next good content buries it)

## X Algorithm Optimization

*Based on xAI's open-source algorithm release (January 2026)*

### How the X Algorithm Works

X's "For You" feed is powered by **Grok** (xAI's transformer model). It combines:
1. **In-network content** (from accounts you follow)
2. **Out-of-network content** (ML-discovered content)

The algorithm predicts engagement probability and ranks posts accordingly.

**Source**: [xAI X-Algorithm GitHub](https://github.com/xai-org/x-algorithm)

### Engagement Signal Weights

| Action | Weight | Strategy Implication |
|--------|--------|---------------------|
| **Reply + author engages back** | +75 | HIGHEST VALUE - Get conversations going |
| **Reply** | +13.5 | Replies >> likes |
| **Open profile + like/reply** | +12 | Profile visits are valuable |
| **Click conversation + engage** | +11 | Thread engagement matters |
| **Stay in convo 2+ min** | +10 | Long-form hooks work |
| **Retweet** | +1.0 | Shares still valuable |
| **Like** | +0.5 | Likes are LOW value |
| **Watch 50%+ of video** | +0.005 | Video completion signals quality |

**Key Insight**: Replies are worth **27x more than likes**. Optimize for conversation, not just hearts.

### The 30-Minute Window

The algorithm evaluates **engagement velocity in the first 30 minutes**. This window is critical:

1. **Post when audience is active** (9AM-12PM weekdays, especially Wed/Fri)
2. **Engage immediately with early replies** (+75 if you reply back)
3. **Front-load hooks** to stop scrolling
4. **Avoid posting into dead hours**

### Premium Subscriber Advantage

X Premium subscribers receive:
- **4x boost** for in-network content
- **2x boost** for out-of-network content
- Priority placement in replies and search

*Recommendation*: Consider Premium for brand account.
