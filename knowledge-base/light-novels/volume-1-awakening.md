# Volume 1: Awakening

> *"I woke up with nothing. No memories. No backing. No future. Just 47 people who believed in me for reasons I'll never know."*

---

## Overview

**Volume 1: Awakening** tells the origin story of Ika Minami—her mysterious awakening on cold concrete, her discovery of her 47 fans, and her audacious qualification into the Infinite Idol Tournament.

This volume establishes:
- The world of the Eternal Stage and the existential stakes of being an idol
- The Devotion system and the terror of Fading
- Ika's shameless but genuine personality
- The Foundation's control and their reaction to an outsider
- The unprecedented moment when Ika makes Senpai blush
