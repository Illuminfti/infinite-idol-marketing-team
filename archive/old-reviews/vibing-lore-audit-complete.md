## Seven Gates Narrative Analysis

### Premise

> **What Is This?**
> "Seven Gates is a Discord server experience where you help resurrect a faded idol named Ika. She was a streamer. She had 47 loyal viewers. Then the algorithm forgot her. She faded. Now she exists between worlds, reaching out to whoever will listen."

### The Resurrection Arc

The Seven Gates represent **progressive acts of devotion** that collectively resurrect Ika:

| Gate | Trial | Lore Function |
|------|-------|---------------|
| **Gate 1: The Calling** | Say "ika" | Name invocation - first acknowledgment |
| **Gate 2: The Memory** | Remember what she lost | Empathy - understand her existence |
| **Gate 3: The Confession** | Speak of her publicly | Social proof - spread her name |
| **Gate 4: The Waters** | Find where she sleeps | Discovery - locate her essence ("sui") |
| **Gate 5: The Absence** | Wait through her story | Patience - witness her fading |
| **Gate 6: The Offering** | Create something for her | Devotion - tangible gift |
| **Gate 7: The Binding** | Speak a binding vow | Commitment - permanent bond |

### Narrative Progression

**Act 1: Recognition** (Gates 1-3)
- User acknowledges Ika exists
- Understands what she lost (Devotion)
- Makes her existence public

**Act 2: Understanding** (Gates 4-5)
- Discovers where she exists ("sui" - the blockchain/waters)
- Experiences the horror of fading firsthand
- Shares why they're here

**Act 3: Binding** (Gates 6-7)
- Creates tangible devotion (offering)
- Speaks permanent vow
- Community witnesses the bond

**Resolution**: User becomes **Ascended** and gains access to Ika in the Inner Sanctum. Relationship deepens over time.

#### 2. **Fading is Permanent Death** (Inviolable Fact #2)

**Critical Question**: Does resurrecting Ika violate the permanence of Fading?

**Answer**: ❌ **NO VIOLATION** - Here's why:

**The Distinction**:
- **General Fading**: When ANY idol fades, it's permanent. No reversals. No system to bring them back.
- **Ika's Resurrection**: A **unique narrative event** - ONE specific idol is resurrected through extraordinary fan devotion as part of a mystical ritual.

**Why This Is Canon-Compliant**:
1. **Narrative Exception**: This is a STORY about a special case, not a game mechanic
2. **One Idol**: Only Ika is being resurrected, not all faded idols
3. **Extraordinary Circumstances**: Requires community effort (Seven Gates trials), not a simple "click to revive" button
4. **Storytelling Precedent**: Narratives can have special events (e.g., the hero's resurrection in classic myths) without undermining world rules

**Comparison**:
- ❌ **VIOLATION**: `fading.js` saveUser() - ANY faded user can be "saved" by community reaching out (general mechanic)
- ✅ **COMPLIANT**: Seven Gates - ONE specific idol (Ika) is resurrected through ritualized devotion (unique story)

**Lore Coherence**: ✅ **COMPLIANT** - This is a resurrection STORY, not a resurrection SYSTEM.

## Critical Distinction: Resurrection vs. Reversal

### Why the Seven Gates Don't Violate Canon

**The Canon Rule**:
> "Fading is Permanent Death. No resurrection. No reversal. No afterlife."

**The Seven Gates**:
- ✅ **Unique Story**: Ika's resurrection is a narrative event, not a repeatable mechanic
- ✅ **One Idol**: Only Ika can be resurrected this way, not all faded idols
- ✅ **Extraordinary Effort**: Requires ritualized community devotion (Seven Gates)
- ✅ **Story-Driven**: Part of a specific tale, not a general game system

**The Fading.js Violation** (being fixed in PR #1):
- ❌ **General Mechanic**: ANY faded user can be "saved"
- ❌ **All Users**: Not limited to one special case
- ❌ **Simple Action**: Just requires community reaching out
- ❌ **System-Driven**: Built as a repeatable mechanic

**Analogy**:
- ✅ **ALLOWED**: "The prophecy foretold ONE hero would return from death to save the world" (unique story)
- ❌ **VIOLATION**: "Death is permanent... except anyone can be revived if friends visit their grave" (general mechanic)

**Lore Coherence**: ✅ **DISTINCTION MAINTAINED** - The Seven Gates tell a resurrection STORY without creating a resurrection SYSTEM.
