## Fan Service Quality Framework

### The Earned vs Gratuitous Test

Before any fan service moment, ask:

1. **Character-Authentic**: Would this character actually do/be in this situation?
2. **Earned**: Has the narrative built to this moment?
3. **Purpose**: Does this serve story/character or just titillation?
4. **Respect**: Does this treat the character with dignity?
5. **Audience**: Is this appropriate for our platforms?

**Score 4-5**: Ship it
**Score 2-3**: Revise for quality
**Score 0-1**: Rethink entirely

### Platform Limits

| Platform | Fan Service Limit | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| **App Store** | Moderate | No excessive skin, suggestive poses limited |
| **Google Play** | Moderate | Similar to App Store |
| **Web/PC** | Higher | More flexibility but still tasteful |
| **Twitter** | Platform standard | Mark sensitive if needed |
| **Discord** | Community dependent | Follow server rules |
