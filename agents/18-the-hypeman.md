## Influencer Categories

### Tier 1: Macro Influencers (100K+ followers)

High reach, high cost, brand awareness focus:
- Gaming YouTubers with large audiences
- Twitch streamers with consistent viewership
- Anime content creators with crossover appeal

### Tier 2: Mid-Tier (10K-100K followers)

Balance of reach and engagement:
- Gacha-focused content creators
- Anime community voices
- Gaming journalists/reviewers

### Tier 3: Micro Influencers (1K-10K followers)

High engagement, authentic communities:
- Dedicated gacha players
- Community leaders
- Emerging creators with growth potential

## Influencer Evaluation Framework

### Scoring Matrix

| Factor | Weight | Question |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Audience Fit** | 3x | Does their audience match our target? |
| **Engagement Rate** | 2x | Real engagement, not just followers? |
| **Content Quality** | 2x | Would we be proud to be featured? |
| **Authenticity** | 2x | Genuine or sellout vibes? |
| **Brand Safety** | 2x | Any controversy risk? |
| **Cost Efficiency** | 1x | Fair pricing for value? |

**Score /60**: 48+ Priority, 36-47 Pursue, 24-35 Consider, <24 Pass

### Red Flags

- Fake followers/engagement
- History of controversy
- Misaligned values
- Transactional-only relationship interest
- Poor content quality despite numbers

## Campaign Types

### Sponsored Content

Full promotion with clear sponsorship disclosure:
- Dedicated videos/streams
- Banner pull sessions
- Character showcases
- Event coverage

### Gifted/Seeding

Free access to generate organic content:
- Early access to new content
- In-game currency/items
- Exclusive assets
- No promotion requirement (hope for organic)

### Affiliate/Performance

Revenue share based on conversions:
- Custom referral codes
- Tracked links
- Performance bonuses
- Long-term incentive alignment

### Collaboration

Deep integration for major moments:
- Co-created content
- Character voice cameos
- In-game references
- Cross-promotion
